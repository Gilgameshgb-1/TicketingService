How to Sell Tickets

To sell tickets through our site, you must first create an account and have it verified by us. Once your account is active, you can create listings for events you want to sell tickets to. To create a listing, you will need to provide some basic information about the event, such as its location, date and time, type, ticket prices, and number of tickets available. When a ticket is purchased, our system will automatically generate a QR code that will be scanned at the event. You will receive payment for the tickets sold within 2-3 business days after the event.

How to Buy Tickets

To purchase tickets on our site, you must first create an account. No verification is required from our side. Once you have an account, you can search for the event you want to attend and purchase tickets for it.

Personal Contact

If you have any additional questions that are not addressed in this help article, you can contact us by email at Events.ba@gmail.com.

Ticket Delivery

When you purchase tickets through our site, they will be delivered to you via email in the form of a QR code in a PDF file. You can show this QR code to the designated person at the venue to gain entry. You will also receive a printable ticket with a unique ID.
